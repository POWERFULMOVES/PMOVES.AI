bar_chart Distribution

The initial offering took place in March 2025. All of one million A0T tokens were minted and are now in circulation.

Decentralized Token Distribution

Agent Zero's token distribution was fully decentralized and managed by the smart contract itself. There was no pre-mine, pre-sale, or airdrops, ensuring fair and transparent access for everyone.

Token Purchase

Tokens were purchased directly from the smart contract through this website. Upon purchase:

  • Tokens were minted by the contract and transferred to the buyer's wallet.
  • The initial token price was set at 0.000200.
  • With each purchase, the price increased by 6.67% of base price 0.000040 (not current or initial price) per 1 ETH spent.

Price Adjustment Mechanism

The price increase was factored into the purchase, meaning buyer paid an average between the current price and the new price. This ensured:

  • No advantage or disadvantage in splitting large purchases into smaller ones.
  • Balanced distribution, preventing early large purchases from dominating the supply.
Example Scenario

Imagine purchasing 100 ETH worth of A0T tokens when current price was 0.000200 per token. After your purchase, the price increased by 6.67 x base price (0.000040) to 0.000467. You would receive tokens at an average price between 0.000200 and ~0.000467 ETH = 0.000315, ensuring a balanced distribution regardless of the purchase size.

Total Supply

The total token supply is hard capped at 1,000,000 tokens.

Funding the Project

ETH collected from token purchases remained in the contract until withdrawn by the project owner to fund Agent Zero's development. To maintain trust and integrity, the smart contract enforced a burning pool mechanism.

Burning pool is a smart contract variable with initial value covering regular withdrawal needs of the project. Burning pool decreases with each ETH withdrawal and increases by burning tokens. When withdrawing ETH, the burning pool was decreased by the amount of A0T tokens corresponding to the withdrawal amount and current minting price.

This mechanism prevented the collected ETH from being reused to purchase more A0T tokens by the project owner.

groups Community Platform

A decentralized ecosystem built around A0T

Our community platform is being built to enable true decentralized governance, identity management, and a foundation for future applications. A0T will serve as both a governance token and a utility token, powering a range of off-chain applications that leverage blockchain for validation, locking, and secure proof ledgers. This ensures flexibility and performance while maintaining transparency and trust.

Decentralized Governance

A0T holders will have direct influence over Agent Zero's future through an open, stake-based governance system. Community proposals can be created, debated, and voted on with tokens staked to ensure authentic participation and prevent manipulation. Blockchain records will act as a tamper-proof ledger for all decisions, ensuring transparency while keeping voting mechanisms efficient and scalable.

On-Chain Identity

The identity system will allow users to link their blockchain address to a unique nickname, making interactions within the ecosystem more intuitive and secure. This decentralized identity framework can be used for governance, locking, and authentication within off-chain applications, ensuring trust without requiring centralized control. Users can maintain anonymity while proving ownership and participation on the blockchain.

Future Applications

A0T is designed to extend far beyond governance and identity. Future applications will run off-chain for efficiency but will utilize blockchain for validation, locking, and proof-of-record. This allows for decentralized AI services, secure reputation systems, verifiable knowledge-sharing platforms, and other innovative use cases. By combining the best of both on-chain security and off-chain flexibility, the Agent Zero ecosystem can scale to meet new demands without compromising trust.
